Temperature and relative humidity are the values you type to set a starting point (dew point follows automatically). From there, each slider moves the point on the chart along a fixed rule: the Temperature slider rides a constant-moisture line (dew point unchanged, RH updates); the Relative Humidity and Dew Point sliders both move moisture content at a constant temperature. Cooling below the current dew point pins relative humidity at 100% and drags the dew point down with it, the way real condensation would.
