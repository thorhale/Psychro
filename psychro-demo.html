Each slider moves the point along a fixed rule: the Temperature slider rides a constant-moisture line (dew point unchanged, RH updates); the Relative Humidity and Dew Point sliders both move moisture content at a constant temperature. Cooling below the current dew point pins relative humidity at 100% and drags the dew point down with it, the way real condensation would.
