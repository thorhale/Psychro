❓ How to Play

Move: WASD, jump with Space. Double-tap Space (or press F) to fly — then Space goes up and Shift goes down.

Build: Right click places the block in your hand, Left click breaks blocks. Pick blocks with 1–9, the mouse wheel, or press E to see every block.

Building Suggestions: press B, choose an idea, then click where you want it to go (R rotates it first). Glowing ghost blocks show the current layer — place matching blocks into them and the next layer appears. Finish the whole thing for a celebration! 🎉

Saving: your world saves itself automatically in this browser.
